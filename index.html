ABOUT ME


Since graduating from Serang Raya University in 2015 and moving to Jakarta in 2018, I have created various portfolios after working with advertising materials. With a unique visual identity that sets the scene apart and with a unique and fun illustration style that extends through the worlds of advertising, art and fashion.

Projects with selected clients: Insurgent Club, Gojek, Maternal Disaster, Marion Jola, THXSMNA, Eka Gustiwana, Smartfren, DWP (2017-2020), Astron Kido, Gozeal and Bearmaze. most of the projects I worked on a Merch, Illustration and Video graphic. I also have a lot of unique art collaborations with several local Indonesian fashion brands.
